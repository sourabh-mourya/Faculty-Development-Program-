About This Program

About FDP 2026

The Faculty of Engineering (Computing & Allied Branches) along with the Department of Computer Science is organising an 8-Day FDP in collaboration with AIU-MU AADC on "Leveraging Artificial Intelligence in Smart Teaching & Learning" from 4th to 11th April 2026 at F Block Minor Auditorium, Medicaps University, Indore, in Hybrid Mode.

Pedagogy Skills
Modern teaching methods, active learning & student engagement strategies.
Research Methods
Literature review, publication strategies, and funding opportunities.
EdTech & AI Tools
AI platforms, LMS integration, and digital content creation for classrooms.
Curriculum Design
Outcome-based education, course mapping, and CO-PO alignment.
NAAC / NBA Prep
Understanding accreditation criteria and documentation practices.
Networking
Connect with peers, mentors, and industry experts across disciplines.

Core Modules

What Will You Learn?

01
Modern Pedagogy & Teaching Strategies
Learner-centric approaches, collaborative learning, case-based teaching, and effective classroom management for higher education.
02
Research Writing & Publication
How to write, format and publish in Scopus/SCI indexed journals. Understanding impact factor, citations, and research funding (DST, SERB).
03
AI-Powered Learning Management
Using AI in LMS platforms, flipped classrooms, personalized learning paths, and automated assessment for engineering disciplines.
04
Smart Content Creation with AI
Creating lecture slides, video content, digital notes, and interactive quizzes using Canva AI, Tome, Gamma, and more.
05
Assessment & Evaluation Design
Constructive alignment, rubric design, question bank creation, continuous evaluation tools, and feedback mechanisms for students.
06
Leadership, IPR & Professional Ethics
Academic leadership, intellectual property rights, patents, industry-academia collaboration, and building a strong research profile.
