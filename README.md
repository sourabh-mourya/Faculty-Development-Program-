# FDP 2026 — Leveraging AI in Smart Teaching & Learning

An informational and registration-linked web platform for the **8-Day Faculty Development Program** organized by the Faculty of Engineering (Computing & Allied Branches) along with the Department of Computer Science, Medicaps University — in collaboration with **AIU-MU AADC**.

**URL**: [https://fdp.medicaps.ac.in/](https://fdp.medicaps.ac.in/)

**Combined Repository**:[https://github.com/StudentDeveloperCommunity/MoonstoneBackend.git](https://github.com/StudentDeveloperCommunity/Faculty-Development-Program-.git)

---

## 📋 Project Overview

### Background

Faculty Development Programs at universities often rely on physical brochures, WhatsApp forwards, and word-of-mouth to reach potential participants. This results in poor discoverability, outdated information, and a scattered registration experience. For FDP 2026, a professional, centralized web presence was needed to reflect the caliber of the program and its speakers.

### Solution Implemented

A fully responsive, single-page informational website was designed and deployed to serve as the official digital face of FDP 2026. The platform consolidates all program information — schedule, resource persons, committee members, core modules, and contact details — into a clean, mobile-friendly interface. Registration is routed directly to the official Medicaps University payment portal, ensuring a reliable and institution-trusted payment flow.

## ✨ Key Features

- **Full program schedule** — day-by-day sessions with speaker names, timings, and hybrid/offline badges
- **Resource persons section** — photo cards for 12+ distinguished speakers from IITs, NITs, and international institutions
- **Committee directory** — chairs, co-chairs, conveners, and technical head listed clearly
- **Core modules overview** — 6 learning themes explained for prospective participants
- **Direct registration link** — routes to the official Medicaps University portal
- **Downloadable brochure** — linked directly from the hero section
- **Responsive navbar** — with hamburger menu for mobile users
- **Contact section** — direct phone links for convener and technical team
- **Hybrid mode indicators** — visual badges distinguishing online vs offline sessions
